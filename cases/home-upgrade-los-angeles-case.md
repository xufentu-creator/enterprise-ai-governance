# Case — Home Upgrade Service (Los Angeles)

AI-assisted decision case for home upgrade services in Los Angeles.

A real-world application of a structured decision approach in remodeling services.

---

## Context

A Los Angeles-based home upgrade service specializing in:

- window and door installation  
- sunroom construction  
- custom cabinets, closets, and storage  

---

## Problem

Traditional remodeling services often face:

- unclear pricing and options  
- heavy reliance on manual explanation  
- customer hesitation before decision  

---

## Solution

A simplified decision flow was introduced:

Scan → Select Problem → Compare Options (A/B/C) → Book Service  

This allows customers to:

- understand their needs clearly  
- compare multiple upgrade solutions  
- make decisions based on budget and preference  

---

## Outcome

- clearer customer decision-making  
- reduced communication time  
- improved conversion rate  

---

## System Note

This case is supported by a structured decision approach for AI-assisted service systems.
