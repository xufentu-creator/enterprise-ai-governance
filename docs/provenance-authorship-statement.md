## Abstract

This document defines the provenance and authorship structure of Xufen Tu’s research on human judgment, decision architecture, structural systems, accountability, and AI-mediated complex environments.

The purpose of this statement is to clarify how authorship continuity, semantic continuity, versioned documentation, public records, and research development are maintained over time.

Provenance is treated here as a structural layer.

It is not only a claim of authorship. It is the traceable relationship between an author, an idea, a public record, a version history, a conceptual structure, and the continued development of a research program.

In AI-mediated environments, ideas can be summarized, replicated, detached from context, recombined, or redistributed at high speed.

For this reason, authorship cannot depend only on a single name, a single file, or a single statement.

This includes:

