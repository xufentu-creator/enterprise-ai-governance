# Enterprise AI Governance Framework

This document defines the enterprise-facing governance layer of the Enterprise AI Governance Advisory repository.

The framework focuses on how organizations structure decisions, responsibilities, and human judgment boundaries in AI-mediated environments.

## Purpose

Enterprise AI governance is not only a technical issue. It is a structural issue.

As AI systems become integrated into organizational workflows, enterprises need clearer structures for:

- decision ownership
- human judgment boundaries
- responsibility distribution
- operational accountability
- digital transformation stability

This framework describes AI governance as a structural coordination problem within complex organizations.

## Core Position

Enterprise AI governance should not be reduced to tool adoption, automation efficiency, or model performance alone.

The central issue is how decisions are shaped, reviewed, escalated, and owned when AI systems participate in organizational processes.
