Cookie Policy

This website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve website functionality and understand how visitors interact with the site.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on a user’s device that allow websites to recognize returning visitors and analyze website usage patterns.

How Cookies Are Used

Cookies may be used for basic website functionality, performance monitoring, or analytics tools that help improve the website experience.

Third-Party Services

Some third-party tools embedded in the website, such as analytics or communication widgets, may place their own cookies according to their respective policies.

Managing Cookies

Users can control or disable cookies through their browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain website functions.
