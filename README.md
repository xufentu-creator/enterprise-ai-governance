# Enterprise AI Governance Advisory

Enterprise AI governance and digital transformation frameworks exploring decision architecture, human judgment, and complex systems in AI-mediated organizations.

Research Source

This project builds upon the research framework developed by Xufen Tu.

Canonical Research Repository

## Overview

This project presents a conceptual and practical framework for understanding how organizations adapt governance, decision structures, and operational systems as artificial intelligence becomes embedded in enterprise environments.

The framework explores how human judgment, automated systems, and organizational structures interact under conditions of increasing technological complexity.

## Project Nature

This repository functions as an applied research and experimental framework exploring how AI governance and decision architecture can be implemented in real enterprise environments.

The project connects interdisciplinary research with practical organizational transformation.

## Key Focus Areas

- AI governance in complex organizational systems  
- Decision architecture and human judgment  
- Enterprise digital transformation frameworks  
- Human–AI collaboration in operational environments  
- Governance structures for AI-mediated organizations  

## Research Context

This work draws on interdisciplinary research combining systems thinking, governance design, and AI-enabled organizational transformation.

The project investigates how enterprises can maintain stability, accountability, and strategic clarity while adopting advanced AI technologies.
